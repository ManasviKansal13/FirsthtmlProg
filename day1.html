The human body is like a kingdom and The soul is its king.But there is a king of all the kings and that is the Supreme Power – God. This is the real beauty of the entire universe.
That is the basis of the universe – and it is like the thread of the garland where you can see the flowers or beads but not the thread which is holding all of them together. In the same way one can see everything but not the one which is holding them intact.
